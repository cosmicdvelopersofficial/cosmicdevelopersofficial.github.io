Could SCP's replace Backrooms Entities?The
Backrooms contain a lot of entities. Could these entities be replaced by SCP's? First of all, what
is a SCP? SCP stands for "Secure, Contain, Protect" this organization has imprisoned all the
abnormal animals, sometimes even alien material and gives them a three-digit code name. For
example, there is an orange "Tickle Monster" with the code SCP-999. Most of the SCP's are
harmful, unlike SCP-999 which can even cure mental trauma. For example the SCP-000 is
friendly called "The Error" (note: the more people know about SCP-001 the stronger it gets). So,
could these monsters replace Backrooms Entities? Some similarities these to categories have are
the following:
They both have harmful and harmless monsters
A huge difference tho is the fact the SCP's can help the human inside the Backrooms. It is also
said that some SCP's are Backrooms Entities that escaped the Backrooms and could be
considered as "The Frontrooms Entities".
